Trilogy

2nd February 2024

SoFi Stadium, Los Angles, United States

Starting from $38.50

Hosted by Illenium

Age Restriction: 18+

Description

After headlining the largest electronic concert in Colorado's history, ILLENIUM is bringing his three-album showcase to Los Angeles for a doubleheader next year.

The melodic bass superstar is set to bring the third "Trilogy" installment to SoFi Stadium, the home of the NFL's Los Angeles Rams and Chargers, in February 2024. As seen in previous iterations of the series, ILLENIUM will take the stage for three distinct sets that encapsulate all of his albums that led him to become one of the biggest artists in modern-day electronic music.

ILLENIUM will take the stage for his next "Trilogy" event on Friday, February 2nd, 2024.
